WURTLE

How to play:

The goal is to correctly guess the 5-letter word. Guess any 5-letter word:

A gray square means the word does not contain that letter.

A yellow square means the word contains that letter, but it is in the wrong position.

A green square means the letter is in the word and in the correct position.
